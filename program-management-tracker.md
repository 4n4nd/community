# Status of efforts important to the Operate First community plan

The status table has cells with the following meanings:
* **Project name**: the interdependency being tracked
* **Status color**: At a glance indication of the status of the project:
  * <p style="color:red; background-color:antiquewhite">RED</p>Project is in trouble, somehow seriously off-track, or significantly behind schedule
  * <p style="color:yellow; background-color:black">YELLOW</p> There is an increased risk of the project milestones being affected.
  * <p style="color:chartreuse; background-color:white">CHARTREUSE</p>: When everything is going well but you have some reservations about calling it all the way Green.
  * <p style="color:green;">GREEN</p>: The project is on-track and on-schedule.
* **Notes** about the status.
